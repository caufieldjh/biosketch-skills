# DOE Biosketch Requirements Checklist

Based on the DOE Biographical Sketch form (Effective 05/01/2025),
OMB-3145-0279 and 1910-0400.

Sources:
- https://ora.stanford.edu/resources/disclosure-resources/department-energy-doe/doe-biosketch-resources
- DOE Biosketch form template (SCV DOE Biosketch v.2025-1)

## General format requirements

- [ ] Header present: "Effective 05/01/2025", "DOE Biosketch",
      "OMB-3145-0279 and 1910-0400"
- [ ] Footer present: "SCV DOE Biosketch v.2025-1" and page numbers
- [ ] Title "BIOGRAPHICAL SKETCH" centered at top
- [ ] Page numbers in "Page X of Y" format

## Identification fields

- [ ] NAME field present (Last, First format)
- [ ] POSITION TITLE field present
- [ ] ORGANIZATION AND LOCATION field present
- [ ] Each field separated by horizontal rules

## Education/Training

- [ ] Section present with header "Education/Training"
- [ ] Formatted as a table with four columns:
  - Institution and Location
  - Degree (if applicable)
  - Completion Date
  - Field of Study
- [ ] All degrees listed (baccalaureate through highest)
- [ ] Each row includes institution name and location
- [ ] Completion dates present for all degrees

### Quality indicators
- Are degrees listed in chronological order?
- Is the field of study specific enough (e.g., "Biochemistry" rather than
  just "Science")?
- Are postdoctoral training periods included?

## Professional Appointments

- [ ] Section present with header "Professional Appointments"
- [ ] Positions listed with dates and institutions
- [ ] Most recent position listed first (reverse chronological)
- [ ] Each entry includes: date range, title, organization, and location
- [ ] Current position clearly indicated (e.g., "present" end date)

### Quality indicators
- Is the date range format consistent (e.g., "2018-present")?
- Are all positions relevant to the individual's qualifications?
- Is the chronology continuous without unexplained gaps?

## Publications

The DOE biosketch organizes publications into two groups:

### Group 1: Most closely related to the proposed project
- [ ] Section present
- [ ] Up to 10 publications listed
- [ ] Each citation includes: authors, title, journal, year, volume/issue/pages
- [ ] DOI or PMID included where available
- [ ] Publications numbered sequentially

### Group 2: Other significant publications
- [ ] Section present
- [ ] Up to 10 publications listed
- [ ] Each citation includes: authors, title, journal, year, volume/issue/pages
- [ ] DOI or PMID included where available
- [ ] Publications numbered sequentially

### Quality indicators
- Are the "closely related" publications actually relevant to the proposed
  project?
- Do the publications demonstrate the methodological expertise needed for
  the proposed work?
- Is the individual's name visible in the author lists?
- Are the publications recent enough to demonstrate current activity?
- Are there enough publications to demonstrate a track record? (Not a strict
  requirement, but reviewers expect evidence of productivity)
- Is the split between "closely related" and "other significant" strategic?
  The most relevant work should be in the first group.

## Synergistic Activities

- [ ] Section present with header "Synergistic Activities"
- [ ] Up to 5 activities listed
- [ ] Activities numbered sequentially
- [ ] Each activity clearly described

### Quality indicators
- Are the activities genuinely synergistic with the proposed work (broader
  impacts, community engagement, mentoring, outreach)?
- Are they specific rather than vague (e.g., "Organized workshop on X for
  Y participants" rather than "Community outreach")?
- Do they demonstrate impact beyond the individual's own research?
- Are they recent and ongoing?

## Common mistakes

1. **Exceeding publication limits** — Maximum 10 per group (20 total)
2. **More than 5 synergistic activities** — Strictly limited to 5
3. **Irrelevant publications** — "Closely related" group should truly relate
   to the proposed project
4. **Incomplete citations** — Missing DOI/PMID, incomplete author lists,
   or missing journal details
5. **Vague synergistic activities** — Should be specific and demonstrable
6. **Missing location information** — Organization and Location field should
   include city, state, and country
7. **Inconsistent date formats** — Should be uniform throughout
8. **Publications spanning both groups** — Same publication should not appear
   in both groups
